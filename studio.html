Our Story

Founded in 2015, Verdi Studio emerged from a simple belief: that the best architecture comes from the marriage of art and science. We saw computational design not as a constraint, but as a catalyst for creativity‚Äîa way to explore possibilities that would be impossible through traditional methods.

Today, we're a team of 24 passionate professionals working at the intersection of architecture, engineering, and environmental science. Every project we undertake is an opportunity to push the boundaries of sustainable design, leveraging cutting-edge tools to create buildings that perform as beautifully as they look.

Our studio culture is built on curiosity, collaboration, and continuous learning. We invest heavily in research, tool development, and knowledge sharing‚Äîboth within our team and with the broader design community.

Our Journey

2015

Studio Founded

Elena Rivera establishes Verdi Studio with a vision to integrate computational tools into sustainable architecture.

2017

First LEED Platinum

Valley Health Clinic achieves LEED Platinum certification, marking our first major sustainability milestone.
